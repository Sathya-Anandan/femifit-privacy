FemiFit Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to FemiFit ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

FemiFit is a fitness tracking app designed for women, helping you sync your workouts and nutrition with your menstrual cycle.

2. Information We Collect

Health Data (via Apple HealthKit)

With your permission, we read the following data from Apple HealthKit:

  • Weight and body measurements
  • Body fat percentage
  • Nutrition information (calories, protein, carbohydrates, fat)
  • Workout and exercise data
  • Step count
  • Sleep analysis
  • Heart rate

Account Information

  • Name and email (via Sign in with Apple, if you choose to share)
  • Apple User ID (for authentication)

Data You Provide

  • Period/menstrual cycle dates
  • Workout plans and schedules
  • Supplement tracking logs
  • Daily calorie goals

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and improve app features
  • Track your fitness and health progress
  • Personalize workout and nutrition recommendations based on your cycle phase
  • Calculate streaks and progress statistics
Important: We do NOT use your health data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing app functionality.

4. How We Protect Your Data

  • Local Storage: All your data is stored locally on your device
  • No External Servers: We do not transmit your health data to external servers
  • iOS Encryption: Your data is protected by iOS's built-in encryption
  • Sign in with Apple: Authentication is handled securely by Apple

5. Data Sharing

We take your privacy seriously:

  • We do NOT sell your data to anyone
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties
  • We do NOT use your health data for advertising purposes

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app
  • Delete: Remove the app to delete all locally stored data
  • Revoke Permissions: Disable HealthKit access at any time via Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → FemiFit
  • Sign Out: Sign out of your Apple ID within the app

7. Children's Privacy

FemiFit is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
